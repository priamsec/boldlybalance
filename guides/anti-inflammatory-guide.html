Inflammation is at the root of most chronic diseases. This guide covers everything you need to know to reduce inflammation and live healthier.

Understanding Inflammation

Chronic inflammation contributes to heart disease, diabetes, arthritis, and faster aging. The good news? Your lifestyle choices can dramatically reduce inflammation.

Quick Start: 5 Anti-Inflammatory Habits

  1. Eat colorful foods – More colors = more antioxidants
  2. Cook with olive oil – Replace processed oils
  3. Eat fish twice a week – Omega-3s fight inflammation
  4. Move daily – Even walking helps
  5. Sleep 7-9 hours – Poor sleep increases inflammation

Foods That Fight Inflammation

  • Fatty fish – Salmon, mackerel, sardines
  • Leafy greens – Spinach, kale, Swiss chard
  • Berries – Blueberries, strawberries
  • Extra virgin olive oil – Rich in oleocanthal
  • Turmeric – Contains curcumin
  • Garlic – Anti-inflammatory compounds
  • Green tea – EGCG antioxidants

Foods to Avoid

  • Sugary drinks and processed foods
  • Trans fats and fried foods
  • Excessive alcohol
  • Processed meats

Supplements That Help

  • Omega-3 fish oil – EPA/DHA for inflammation
  • Vitamin D – Most people are deficient
  • Magnesium – Helps with sleep and inflammation
  • Curcumin – Anti-inflammatory compound

The Bottom Line

Reducing inflammation doesn't require perfection. Start with one change, build consistency, and add more habits over time. Your body will thank you.
