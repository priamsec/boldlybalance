Supplements (Worth Every Penny)

Not tech, but these work better than most gadgets:

  • Magnesium Glycinate – $15-25, take 200-400mg before bed
  • Glycine – $10-15, 3g helps you fall asleep

What to Skip

  • Expensive "smart" mattresses (overpriced)
  • Fancy sleep headsets (comfort issues)
  • Most sleep supplements with long ingredient lists

The Bottom Line

Start with basics: blackout curtains, cool room, magnesium. Then add tech if you want data. The best sleep tool is still consistency—not gadgets.
